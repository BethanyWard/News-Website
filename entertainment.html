    • Robert Pattinson is a FRAUD?!

    Robert Pattinson, famous for his role of 'Edward' in the Twilight series, shocks fans around the world by announcing "I'm not actually a vampire, guys." This revelation, coupled with the fact that he seems unharmed by the sun and has been seen regularly enjoying garlic bread, has devastated entire communities.

    Following Robert's comment, one man (who wished to remain anonymous) said "Hey, that's the guy who plays Batman." Unfortunately, it seems Robbie is no 'bat-man' after all.

    Is Morbius to Blame for Rising Gas Prices?

    After its less than successful release, the movie "Morbius" has been an easy target for jokes. However, this failed blockbuster is so much more than a laughing stock: as gas prices increase around the world, you and others might have briefly thought 'It's that darn Morbius's fault.' After doing no research, a team of scientists decided that yes, it is that darn Morbius's fault. Could a failed Marvel movie truly be the cause for such a big economic problem? No one knows, because they're not willing to watch the movie to find out.
